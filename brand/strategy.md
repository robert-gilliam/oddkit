# oddkit — Strategy Brief

> Generated on 2026-03-26. Version: 1. Status: working draft, not a commitment.

## Purpose

Give agents what they need to do great work. Understand the business logic first, keep the guardrails lightweight, ship code that humans can actually read.

## Audience

Senior devs with strong opinions. Frustrated by two failure modes: cowboy AI coding with zero guardrails, and process-heavy tooling that recreates the bureaucracy they already hated. They want agentic development to work in practice, not in theory.

## Philosophy

- Business logic first. Figure out what the humans want before the agent starts building.
- Less is more. Compact skills, simple code, not much of it.
- Guardrails serve the agent, not the developer's ego. Rules exist to wrangle agents, not to perform process.
- Start small, build up. No premature architecture. No specs that try to define everything upfront.
- Ceremony is the enemy. Stale docs, configuration theater, overwrought frameworks — all out.

## Voice

- Say the thing, then stop. High information density, no filler.
- Human, not polished. Lowercase energy. No marketing voice.
- Never perform cleverness. Wit only when it's free.
- Avoid LLM cliche patterns. No "it's not X, it's Y." No em-dash laden manifestos. No scope creep in copy.
- The brand practices what it builds. If the tool is compact, the messaging is compact.

## Anti-patterns

- Stale documentation as a substitute for clear thinking
- Configuration theater (47 knobs when you need 3)
- Premature architecture that doesn't solve the core problem
- Process that exists to make humans feel safe instead of making agents perform well
- Overwrought language, excessive sentence fragments, fluff

## Working Brand Concept

"odd" as a direction, not a lock. The oddness is structural: in a space full of over-engineered frameworks and bloated process, being genuinely minimal is the contrarian move. Opinionated tools that make choices for you, packaged simply, without ceremony.

Name, logo (floating eyeball with wings), and visual identity are all provisional. Let the product lead. Revisit once there are real users reacting to real work.

## Brand Essence

Compact, opinionated workflow skills for agentic development. Clarity over ceremony. GSD with just enough structure.
